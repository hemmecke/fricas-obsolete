BalancedPAdicRational p¶

padic.spad line 524 [edit on github]

  • p: Integer

Stream-based implementation of Qp: numbers are represented as sum(i = k.., a[i] * p^i), where the a[i] lie in -(p - 1)/2, …, (p - 1)/2.
